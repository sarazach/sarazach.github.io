About the Couple



Zachary grew up in Apex, NC and will be graduating from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in May 2015 with a degree in Chemistry. He hopes to pursue a degree in Pharmacy after graduation. He is currently working as a barista for Starbucks in Chapel Hill.

Sara grew up in Holly Springs, NC and will be graduating from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in May 2015 with a degrees in Management and Society and Economics. She is currently an assistant store manager for Starbucks in Cary.

Zachary and Sara met freshman year of high school, at the homecoming football game. Kevan Schoonover and Zachary had been lifelong friends, and Sara was hanging out with Kevan at the football game. Kevan introduced the two, and after a month of getting to know each other, they began dating.

Zachary proposed to Sara on September 13, 2014 at Wrightsville Beach, NC, at the mailbox near Shell Island Resort. Kevan Schoonover and Sam Turner helped to document the moment, and Karen James helped to make it possible.
